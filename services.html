Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel for projects? +

Yes! We travel nationwide and internationally for projects. Whether your event is in Nairobi or beyond, we're equipped to deliver the same level of excellence wherever you are.

What's included in your service packages? +

Packages vary by service and scope. Corporate events include on-site shooting, post-production retouching, and digital delivery. Drone services offer aerial footage and high-resolution images. All packages include consultation and customized deliverables. Get in touch for specific details.

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines depend on the service. Corporate events are same-day to 2-week turnaround for final edits. Drone surveys typically take 2-4 weeks. 3D mapping and complex projects may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Are custom packages available? +

Absolutely! We specialize in custom solutions. Our team will work with you to create a tailored package that fits your budget and vision. No two projects are the same, and neither should your quote be.

Do you offer print services? +

Yes! We provide high-quality prints for corporate events and portfolio work. Speak with us about your specific printing needs during the consultation phase.
